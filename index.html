All About Me
This webpage is to share with you a little about me. I am an educator who has been teaching for the last 18 years. I live in the Caribbean and love island life! Read on to learn more about me.

About Me

My name is Gwen Gibbes and I am an elementary school teacher. I have been teaching for 18 years and have taught all grades from Grade 1 to 11. I am currently teaching grade 3 and have been doing so exclusively online for the last 2 years. This past March was the 2 year anniversary of online teaching. It has been so different from anything I had ever experienced prior to online teaching.

Career Goals

Until recently I thought I would teach forever and while I may still, I have felt it was time to learn a new skill. My current goal is to learn enough about coding and web design to properly understand how to code a website. I want to be able to create efficient and appealing websites. I would like to understand how to turn this new knowledge into a career.

Interests

I am interested in constantly learning and evolving. Keeping up with the ever-changing world is something that fascinates me. I am always in awe of the new things, both positive and negative, that we can learn. I am also interested in the environment and making sure that we take care of the Earth that we live on.

Animals

My hobbies are outdoor adventures and anything to do with animals. I absolutely adore animals and over the 2 years that we were locked down due to Covid, our family adopted a cat. He found us really. His name was Squeak and he appeared on our porch one evening. He made our home his for 9 months and I like to believe that even though he was a feral cat, he really loved staying with us.
